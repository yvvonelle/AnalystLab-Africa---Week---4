# Week 4: Data Visualization & Dashboarding
** AnalystLab Africa Data Analytics Internship 

---

## Overview
This project involves building an interactive business dashboard using **Microsoft Power BI** on the Superstore Sales Dataset to communicate key business insights through data visualization.

---

## Dataset
- **Name:** Superstore Sales Dataset
- **Rows:** 9,995 | **Columns:** 21
- **Source:** Retail sales transactions across US regions

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Microsoft Excel (data preview)
- Microsoft Word & PowerPoint (documentation)

---

## KPIs Defined
| KPI | Value |
|-----|-------|
| Total Revenue | $2.30M |
| Total Profit | $286.40K |
| Total Orders | 9,994 |
| Profit Margin | 12.47% |
| Total Quantity Sold | 38,000 units |
| Top Region | West ($725K) |

---

## Visualizations Built
- KPI Cards — Sales, Profit, Orders, Margin, Quantity
- Bar Chart — Sales by Category
- Line Chart — Monthly Sales Trend
- Pie/Donut Chart — Sales by Segment
- Map Visual — Sales by State
- Bar Chart — Top Products by Sales

## Dashboard Features
- 4 interactive slicers: Region, Segment, Category, Order Date
- Cross-filtering across all visuals

---

## Key Insights
1. **Technology** is the top revenue category at $0.84M
2. **November** peaks at $352K — nearly 6x February's low
3. **Consumer segment** leads with $1.16M in sales
4. **West region** generates the highest revenue at $725K
5. **Profit margin of 12.47%** signals room to improve discount strategies
